About Mythsnare

Fighting misinformation with cutting-edge AI technology

Our Mission

Mythsnare is an advanced AI-powered fact-checking system designed to combat misinformation and help users distinguish between factual information and false claims. In an era where misinformation spreads rapidly across digital platforms, we provide a reliable, intelligent solution to verify claims and news in real-time.

Our mission is to empower individuals, organizations, and communities with the tools they need to make informed decisions based on accurate information, thereby contributing to a more trustworthy and transparent information ecosystem.

How It Works

1

Input Your Query

Enter your claim, statement, or question through text, voice recording, or by uploading audio/video/document files. Our system accepts multiple input formats for maximum flexibility.

2

AI Classification

Our BERT-based machine learning model analyzes your input to determine whether it's a factual claim or news-related content. This classification helps us apply the most appropriate verification strategy.

3

Multi-Source Verification

For news content, we extract key entities and cross-reference with real-time news sources from Google News RSS feeds and Wikipedia. For factual claims, we leverage our LLaMA AI model for deep analysis.

4

Intelligent Analysis

Our LLaMA 3.1 model processes all gathered information, applies critical reasoning, and provides a confident assessment with detailed explanation and confidence score.

5

Receive Results

Get a clear verdict (True/False), confidence percentage, and comprehensive explanation. Our results are presented in an easy-to-understand format with visual indicators for quick comprehension.
